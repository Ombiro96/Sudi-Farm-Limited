Sudi Farm

Welcome to Sudi Farm Limited where agriculture is not only our job but our Lifestyle.
Sudi Farm Limited is an organisation that proudly focuses on the future of Kenyan agriculture. We live and work in the regions and represent the interests and aspirations of farmers and rural communities.

What We Do ...

We are an agribusiness company in Kenya that specializes in large-scale livestock and crop farming. .We currently sell the produce within the region i.e county but we want to expand our customer base to be national i.e country-wide.
Some of our current customers include supermarkets, hotels, schools, colleges and universities, butcheries, food processors, and individuals who buy in bulk. We pride ourself in our exceptionally high-quality produce.

Our story

Sudi Farm Limited started off as an a small scale sole proprietorship by the now current owner Mr.Kamau with the help of his wife Mrs.Kamau who had great passion in agriculture and animal farming in the year 1997. His Love for animals and passion for farming encouraged him to keep pushing and leveling up the business, through his skills and interpersonal communication with his employees the business was nurtured into something incredible.

Mr Kamau is now a proud owner of one of the largest firms in county and soon aiming for international standards. Sudi Farm Ltd.
